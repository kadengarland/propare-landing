4. Services Provided

Propare allows athletes to browse, review, book, and participate in online coaching sessions. Coaches can create profiles, set pricing, and manage availability. Propare acts solely as a facilitator and is not a party to any coaching relationship or transaction between athlete and coach. Coaches are independent contractors, not employees, agents, or representatives of Propare. All sessions must be conducted remotely and outside university facilities.

Session Types

Live video sessions: Real-time one-on-one coaching conducted through Microsoft Teams or other Propare-approved video tools. The athlete books a time, pays, and receives a session link automatically.

Video analysis sessions: The athlete uploads a single training clip and the coach reviews it using Propare's built-in tools. The coach provides timestamped feedback, corrections, and coaching notes. Once the coach delivers the session it locks permanently and becomes a replay-only record for the athlete. Coaches must deliver video analysis feedback within 48 hours of the athlete submitting their clip.

Session Records

Completed session data including feedback, voice notes, drawings, and video analysis is stored on the platform and accessible to the athlete and coach involved in that session. Session history is visible on the athlete's profile as a record of completed coaching activity.

Propare reserves the right to adjust platform fees in the future. Coaches will receive at least 14 days written notice before any fee changes take effect.
